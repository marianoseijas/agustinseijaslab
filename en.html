My name is Agustin Eduardo Seijas and my products are my initials. For over thirty-five years I have dedicated to audio.

During this time I specialized in the service of major brands worldwide, including: Krell and Mark Levinson, who trusted in my ability, backed by my personal high-tech laboratory. Simultaneously my research was aimed at studying the different topologies or ways to amplify the sound. In this way, after testing, i found in the electronic valve the best way to get the sound I expected.

Thus were born these Dinamika, Kometa, ELFO II and UNO Preamplifier.

For more information: seijas.asl@gmail.com
